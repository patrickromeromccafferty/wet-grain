Wet Grain is an annual print magazine for new poetry in English. Its fifth issue, guest-edited by Leo Boix & Nat Teitler FRSL, 
spotlighted the work of British Latinx poets.

Since 2020 has included the work of superb emerging poets alongside recipients of awards including the Pulitzer, a MacArthur Fellowship, 
the Pushcart Prize, the German Book Prize, and the Eric Gregory. 

Each issue, selected poets are invited to contribute a short piece of T covers are hand-printed with original designs.
